The Youth Ministry

Giving purpose for middle schoolers and high schoolers.

Advertisement

Giving purpose for middle schoolers and high schoolers.

We call our youth ministry the “Y” ministry because we focus on teaching our youth the reason why we do everything we do. We do not want to pass on only behavioral rules and neglect the greater reasoning behind all that God has taught us. We want our high school and middle school students to ask why and seek after a deep understanding of God.

Our Y ministry meets in informal small groups all around the Nigeria for Bible discussions and activities and community service events. In addition, we hold annual devotionals that bring together students from high schools located around Nigeria.
