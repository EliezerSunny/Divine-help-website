OUR DREAM.

The mission of the O.I.M C&S DIVINE HELP CHURCH is to be on co-mission with Christ to "go and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit." (Mathew 28:19-20) To be faithful to the call of God. We continue to build a caring loving, community within our congregation that reaches out to those around us.

The vision of O.I.M C&S DIVINE HELP CHURCH is to sustain growing relationships with Jesus Christ.

We believe our core value create and define us as a congregation serving our Lord and Savior, Jesus Christ. We celebrate these values using the acronym GRACE:

  1. Give joyously
  2. Reach others in community through christ
  3. Assist through serving with our unique gifts
  4. Care for our church family
  5. Equip one another for ministry
